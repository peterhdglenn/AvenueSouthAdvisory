About

The operator's perspective. The advisor's discipline.

Avenue South Advisory was built on a simple premise: the best consulting doesn’t come from a slide deck — it comes from someone who has run an operation, managed the team, and been accountable for the outcome.

That’s the experience Anthony Viscichini brings to every engagement. Over a 25-year career spanning FedEx Ground, Express Couriers International, and Amazon, he has led operations at every level — from the dock floor to the executive table. He knows what it looks like when a supply chain is quietly bleeding margin, when a team has outgrown its processes, and when a company is scaling faster than its infrastructure can support. More importantly, he knows how to fix it.

What sets Avenue South Advisory apart is the combination of strategic clarity and operational depth. Anthony doesn’t arrive with a generic framework and a polished presentation. He gets into the work — understanding your business, identifying where the real leverage is, and building solutions that are practical, durable, and designed to perform without him in the room.

The approach is direct, the thinking is data-informed, and the focus is always on creating meaningful change — not just incremental improvement. Whether the engagement calls for untangling a complex supply chain, restructuring an organization for scale, or bringing AI and smarter process design into the mix, Avenue South Advisory is built to deliver results that last.
